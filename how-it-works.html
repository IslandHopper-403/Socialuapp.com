Frequently Asked Questions

Is SocialU really free?
Yes! SocialU is 100% free for users. We make money by offering premium features to businesses, not by selling your data or showing annoying ads.
How do you ensure safety?
We take safety seriously. All profiles are verified, we have robust reporting and blocking features, and our team monitors for suspicious activity 24/7. Your conversations are private and secure.
Can I use SocialU for dating?
While SocialU isn't exclusively a dating app, many users have made romantic connections! We focus on genuine community connections of all kinds - friendships, activity partners, networking, and yes, dating too.
What if there aren't many users in my area yet?
We're growing fast! You can help by inviting friends and local businesses you love. Early adopters in new areas get special perks and badges to recognize their support.
How do businesses get on SocialU?
Businesses can sign up easily and create profiles for free. They can post promotions, connect with customers, and build their local community. Premium features are available for enhanced visibility.
Can I delete my account anytime?
Absolutely. You have full control over your account and data. You can delete your account anytime from settings, and all your data will be permanently removed.
