üåü Why Visit Hoi An?

Hoi An is not your typical Vietnamese city. This UNESCO World Heritage site perfectly blends ancient charm with modern backpacker culture, creating a unique atmosphere that keeps travelers coming back year after year.

Here's what makes Hoi An special:

  • Car-free Ancient Town: Explore narrow streets lined with 15th-century architecture without dodging motorbikes
  • Incredible Food Scene: From $1 street food to Michelin-worthy restaurants
  • Proximity to Paradise: Beaches, rice paddies, and waterfalls all within 30 minutes
  • Thriving Social Scene: Perfect for solo travelers looking to make friends
  • Affordable Everything: Stretch your budget further than almost anywhere in Southeast Asia

‚úàÔ∏è Getting to Hoi An

By Air

The closest airport is Da Nang International Airport (DAD), located 30km from Hoi An (about 45 minutes by car).

From Da Nang Airport to Hoi An:

  • Grab/Taxi: 300,000-400,000 VND ($12-16 USD) - Most convenient
  • Airport Shuttle: 150,000 VND ($6 USD) - Runs every 2 hours
  • Local Bus: 50,000 VND ($2 USD) - Take Bus #1 to Da Nang, then local bus to Hoi An

By Bus/Train

Vietnam has excellent bus networks connecting Hoi An to other major cities:

  • From Hanoi: 16-20 hours, $25-35 USD (sleeper bus)
  • From Ho Chi Minh: 20-24 hours, $25-35 USD (sleeper bus)
  • From Hue: 3-4 hours, $5-8 USD
  • From Nha Trang: 10-12 hours, $15-20 USD
